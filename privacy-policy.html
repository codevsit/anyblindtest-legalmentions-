1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects and processes limited information when you use it. This details what is collected and why:

  • Device & System Information: We collect device models, operating system versions, and basic diagnostic information to maintain application stability and performance.
  • Media Metadata: The Application temporarily accesses media metadata (song titles, artists, albums) via notification listener permissions. This data is processed strictly on your device to drive the blind test gameplay and sync with Wear OS. It is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.
  • Audio & Voice Commands: The Application requests access to the microphone (`RECORD_AUDIO`) to allow speech recognition for guessing song titles. All audio processing is handled **locally on-device** (or via standard system speech APIs). No audio recordings are saved, stored, or sent to our servers.
  • Advertising ID: The Application uses the Google Advertising ID (`AD_ID`) to show ads through AdMob and measure ad performance in compliance with Google Play Policies.

The Application utilizes third-party services that process data according to their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Since the Application does not require account creation, we do not store personal profiles on external databases. All gameplay history is stored locally on your device. You can erase this data at any time by clearing the Application's storage in your system settings or by uninstalling the Application.

4. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Technologies

The Application incorporates local AI technologies to provide certain features. All AI processing is performed in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable laws. No personal data is used to train AI models.

5. Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will immediately delete it. If you are a parent or guardian aware of such data sharing, contact us at codevsit@proton.me.

6. Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We implement procedural and electronic safeguards to secure local and aggregated data.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us via email at codevsit@proton.me.
